The Current Pokemon Format

This format is one of the official formats for tournaments

It is played in double battles, and the format all of the teams I will be showing are based on


These are some examples of the teams that were built.

This was a team built by Wolfe Glick

This team works on the rain, and perish trap. The rain part of the team consists of politoed as the rain setter with the ability drizzle, and kingdra and Archaludon as damage dealers that work best in the rain. The perish trap consists of politoed as the one who uses perish song and gothitelle as the one who traps opponent pokemon in with the move shadow tag. The incineroar and amoongus act as support for the rest of the team.

This was a team built by Dimitri Koziaris

The team has porygon to set up trick room and kilowatrel to set up tailwind depending on the situation. The gholdengo and ursaluna are there for damage. Incineroar and Amoongus are there for support.

This was a team built by Oliver Ramsay

Blood moon Ursaluna, Gholdengo, and Sneasler are meant for damage. Dragonite, Incineroar, and Amoongus are meant for support.

This was a team built by Chih-Hung Chu

This team uses the sun, trick room, and psychic terain. Drought is set up by torkoal, and used by chlorophyl solar blade liligant. Indeedee and galade can be used to set up trick room, which is used by the entire team. Indeedee is also used to set up psychic terain which is used by expanding force hatterene. Ursaluna takes good use of trick room.
